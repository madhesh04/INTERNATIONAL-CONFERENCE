About This Events

International Conference on Sustainable Food - Water - Energy - Mechanization - Nexus and Whole Grain

Wanna Know Our Mission?

The International Conference aims to bring together technocrats to establish a scientific network sharing recent ... advancements in Agricultural Engineering and Allied Sciences. Organized by the Agricultural Engineering College and Research Institute, Tamil Nadu Agricultural University, in collaboration with NIFTEM-T, Thanjavur, and the University of Guelph, Canada, the mission is to empower researchers and professionals in sustainable food, water, energy, and mechanization sectors.

What you will learn?

Participants will gain insights into cutting-edge research and advancements in Agricultural Engineering and Food Technology. ... Topics include resource management, food and fuel crisis solutions, mechanization innovations, and sustainable practices. The event provides a platform for students, researchers, and industry professionals to learn from esteemed institutes like NIFTEM-T and the University of Guelph, focusing on modern technologies and value addition in agriculture and food processing.

What are the benefits?

Attendees will benefit from networking with global experts and gaining access to incubation facilities and research opportunities. ... Institutions like NIFTEM-T and TNAPEx aim to empower stakeholders—farmers, rural youth, women, and entrepreneurs—by promoting value addition, quality excellence, and export competitiveness. Participants will also learn from leaders like Dr. K. Alagusundaram, whose initiatives enhance food security and modernize agricultural practices.

Nearest Tourist Places

Explore the nearby attractions during your visit to the conference.

SRIRANGAM

• The temple is said to be the largest temple in India with the largest religious complexes in the world.

• Built-in the Dravidian architecture style, the temple has 108 deities displaying different forms of Vishnu.

• Srirangam is considered a holy island, surrounded by the Kaveri River and its tributary, the Kollidam, adding to its spiritual significance.

THANJAVUR BIG TEMPLE

• The Brihadeeswarar Temple is an architectural marvel and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

• It was built by the Chola King Raja Raja Chola I in the 11th century and is dedicated to Lord Shiva.

• The temple features a 216-foot vimana (tower) and a massive Nandi statue carved from a single stone.

KALLANAI DAM

• Kallanai is an ancient dam built by Karikala of the Chola dynasty in 150 CE.

• The dam is the fourth oldest dam in the world and the first in India.

• It continues to serve as a vital irrigation structure, showcasing ancient engineering brilliance.

International Conference - 2025

What is the purpose of this website?
This website is dedicated to providing information about the International Conference 2025, including schedules, speakers, pricing, sponsors, and nearby tourist attractions for visitors to explore.
Where is the conference located?
The conference will be held at Agricultural Engineering College and Research Institute, Tamil Nadu Agricultural University, Kumulur. Detailed directions and a Google Maps link are provided on the site for your convenience.
How can I register for the conference?
You can register for the conference by visiting the "Submit" section on the website and filling out the registration form with your details.
Are there any tourist places near the conference venue?
Yes! There are several beautiful tourist places nearby: • Srirangam Temple: Known for its Dravidian architecture and as one of the largest temples in India. • Thanjavur Big Temple: A UNESCO World Heritage site showcasing Chola dynasty architecture. • Kallanai Dam: An ancient engineering marvel built by the Chola dynasty.
What facilities are provided during the conference?
The conference offers: • Access to all sessions and workshops. • Refreshments and lunch during the event. • A welcome kit with conference materials. • Networking opportunities with industry experts and scholars.
How can I become a sponsor for the conference?
If you're interested in sponsoring the event, please visit the "Sponsors" section on the website and contact us using the provided details.
Can I download the conference brochure?
Yes, you can download the conference brochure by clicking on the "Get Brochure" button in the main carousel section.
Is accommodation provided for participants?
Accommodation is not included in the registration fee. However, we recommend nearby hotels, and details can be shared upon request.
